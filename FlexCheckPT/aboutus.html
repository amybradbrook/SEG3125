YOUR PERSONAL TRAINER

JORDON BLAKE

Jordan Blake is a certified personal trainer with over 8 years of experience helping clients achieve their fitness goals. With a background in exercise science and nutrition, Jordan specializes in strength training, weight loss, and injury recovery. After graduating with a degree in Kinesiology from the University of Ottawa, Jordan began working with clients of all ages and fitness levels, from beginners to professional athletes. Jordan tailors every workout plan to suit your individual needs and goals.
